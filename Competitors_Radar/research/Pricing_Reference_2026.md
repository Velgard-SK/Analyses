## Key Pricing Insights

1. **Mandatory platform fees**: Synack charges $16,000/yr platform subscription on top of all pentest engagement fees — effectively enterprise-only despite per-engagement pricing
2. **Per-scanner vs per-asset**: Tenable Nessus licenses per scanner (unlimited IPs per scanner); Qualys and Rapid7 license per asset — very different economics at scale
3. **Credits model (Cobalt)**: 1 credit = 8 hrs AI+human testing; credits don't roll over — incentivizes annual purchasing but limits flexibility
4. **Flat-fee advantage (Aikido)**: Aikido's flat monthly fee ($300–$1,050/mo) regardless of user/repo/asset count is a major differentiator vs per-developer/per-asset vendors
5. **MDR included (Cynet)**: Cynet includes 24/7 MDR (CyOps) at no extra charge — rare; most XDR vendors charge $3–$8/endpoint/mo extra for MDR
6. **EU compliance entry (DataGuard)**: Only EU-native GRC SaaS with public starting price (€175/mo); US GRC platforms (Vanta, Drata) all require custom quotes
7. **Pay-per-finding (bug bounty)**: YesWeHack, Intigriti, Zerocopter all use pay-per-valid-finding + platform fee; actual cost depends on researcher activity — total can range $5K to $500K+/yr
8. **Compliance audit fees are separate**: Vanta and Drata platform fees cover the compliance tooling — the actual ISO 27001 / SOC 2 audit ($10K–$100K+) is completely separate
